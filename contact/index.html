What our
Collaboration Will Look Like
Reach Out

You send me a message with your project details or ideas. We will briefly discuss what you need and clarify the main goals of the project to ensure we are on the same page.

Get-to-Know Meeting

We will have a meeting where I will ask questions about your project and goals. You will also share your expectations, and together we will outline the scope of work to understand exactly what needs to be done.

Onboarding

In this step, I will explain the process, tools, and timelines we will follow. I will make sure you understand how we will work together and what’s required to get started, so we both know what to expect.

Project Work Begins

With everything in place, I will start the actual work on your project. During this phase, I will focus on the tasks at hand while keeping you updated on progress, and we will adjust anything if needed as we go along.

Follow-Up Meeting

After some work is done, we will have a meeting to review everything so far. You will have a chance to give feedback, ask any questions, and we will make any changes needed based on your input.

Handover Meeting

In the final meeting, I will present the finished project and go over how everything works. I will provide the necessary files and documents, and we will make sure all the details are clear so you can move forward with what’s been delivered.
