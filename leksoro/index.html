About the project

This was a freelance project focused on designing a new digital product that helps people find the right personal fitness trainer. The goal was to simplify the search and booking process while improving trainer visibility and engagement. The platform supports personalized discovery based on user needs, verified credentials, and clear pricing. Built with scalability in mind, it allows for easy expansion across cities, gyms, and networks.

The problem

Finding a personal trainer can be frustrating filters are often unclear, prices aren’t transparent, and personalization is lacking. Without verified credentials or reviews, trust is low. Plus, the communication and booking process is usually scattered across multiple platforms, creating unnecessary friction.

Industry

The fitness industry is growing fast, with increasing demand for tailored coaching. But the market is highly competitive. Trainers struggle to stand out, and clients struggle to choose. Pricing differs by experience, session type, and location. Certification and credibility matter users are more likely to trust and hire verified professionals.

User groups

The platform is built for two main user groups: Personal Trainers – Professionals with a unique style, specialized training methods, and a desire to grow their client base. They use the platform to present their approach, showcase credentials, and get discovered by people looking for exactly what they offer. Clients – Individuals searching for a personal trainer who fits their goals, budget, and preferences. They expect transparency clear pricing, verified qualifications, reviews, and a simple way to connect and book sessions without back-and-forth communication.

Research
Methodology

To understand the needs of both personal trainers and clients, I adopted a task-based approach, focusing on how each group interacts with the platform to achieve their goals. The research involved sorting and defining tasks specific to each user type. I conducted interviews with personal trainers to gather direct insights about their challenges, motivations, and expectations. This helped shape a platform designed to connect trainers and clients smoothly while addressing common pain points like communication and trust.

User Interviews

I spoke directly with personal trainers to understand how they market themselves, what makes them stand out, and how they prefer to be contacted. This helped identify key selling points like verified credentials and training styles.

User Flow Observation

I analysed how people currently search for trainers, where they get stuck, what filters they use, and how long it takes to make a decision. This shaped a smoother, task-based search and booking flow.

Persona Mapping

I created two key user types trainers and clients outlining their goals, frustrations, and decision factors. This ensured the platform meets the real needs of both sides, not just one.

Task Analysis

I broke down each step a user takes from discovering a trainer to booking them and simplified each task. This reduced friction and ensured the platform supports both quick actions and deeper exploration.

Design process

The design process created a clear, intuitive flow starting from user entry to the main listing page, where all personal trainers are displayed. From there, users can easily search, start conversations, and connect directly with trainers. This seamless flow supports both trainers and clients, making the platform efficient and user-friendly for both sides.

How it is used

The platform allows users to discover and connect with fitness trainers based on their specific needs. Users can filter by location, type of training, certifications, availability, and price. This eliminates guesswork and shortens the time to decision. For trainers, the platform boosts visibility and connects them with clients who fit their expertise.

Conclusion

This platform effectively bridges the gap between personal trainers and clients by catering to the specific needs of both user groups. It offers trainers a way to showcase their skills and methods, while providing clients with a clear, easy-to-use system to find and book the right trainer. The design and functionality are based on thorough research, including interviews and task analysis, ensuring the platform delivers a seamless and efficient user experience for all.
